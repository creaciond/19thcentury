Annotation

As in any other corpus intended for linguistic and sociological research, the texts of the Corpus are supplemented with linguistic and metalinguistic information, or markup. Linguistic markup includes morphological markup and error markup. Currently, the texts of the corpus contain metatextual markup and morphological markup.
